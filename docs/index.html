What it does.

Everything a Cambodian professional needs, nothing more.

Khmer lunar dates

Gregorian to Chhankitek conversion with waxing and waning moon phases on every day.

Public holidays

All Cambodian holidays — fixed and lunar-computed. Countdown to the next one.

Working day status

See if today is a working day, weekend, or public holiday at a glance.

Buddhist era

Buddhist year, 12-year animal cycle, and Sak era displayed clearly.

Custom reminders

Set reminders on any date. Get notified at the time you choose.

Menu bar formats

Choose Khmer numeral, dual format, lunar date, full Khmer, or icon only.

Right-click to copy

Copy Khmer dates, Gregorian dates, holidays, or full info to clipboard.

Keyboard navigation

Arrow keys for months. T for today. Y for year view. Escape to go back.

Settings

Configure menu bar format, notifications, reminders, and launch at login.

Native & lightweight

Built with SwiftUI. Instant launch, no battery drain, launches at login.
